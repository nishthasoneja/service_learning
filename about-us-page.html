About Us

Learn about our mission and work in cow protection

Our Story

Gayathri Naatti Hassu Goshala Charitable Trust was established in 2015 with a mission to provide care, protection, and shelter for cows in need. Located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, our Goshala has grown from humble beginnings to become a sanctuary for hundreds of cows.

The Goshala was founded by a group of dedicated individuals who were concerned about the welfare of abandoned, old, and injured cows. Their vision was to create a safe haven where these sacred animals could live with dignity and receive proper care.

Over the years, with the support of generous donors and volunteers, we have expanded our facilities and improved our capabilities to provide better care for our bovine residents.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate, and provide lifetime care for abandoned, injured, and old cows, while promoting awareness about cow protection and its importance in Indian culture and heritage.

Vision

A world where every cow is treated with respect and compassion, where their contribution to ecology and human welfare is recognized, and where they can live their lives with dignity without fear of neglect or cruelty.

Cow Breeds

Sahiwal

Sahiwal cows are known for their high milk production and adaptability to hot climates.

Kankrej

Kankrej cows are famous for their strength and endurance, often used as draft animals.

Red Sindhi

Red Sindhi cows are known for their resilience and good milk yield even in harsh conditions.

Tharparkar

Tharparkar cows are well-adapted to arid regions and are valued for both milk and draft purposes.

Hallikar

Hallikar cows are strong draft animals native to Karnataka, known for their endurance and strength.

Malnad Gidda

Malnad Gidda is a small-sized, indigenous breed from Karnataka, highly resistant to diseases.

Amrithmahal

Amrithmahal cows are known for their speed, strength, and ability to work in agricultural fields.

Deoni

Deoni cows are dual-purpose breeds valued for both milk production and draft work.

Gir

Gir cows are well known for their high milk yield and are widely used in dairy farming.

Ongole

Ongole cows are sturdy and muscular, known for their resilience and used in breeding programs.

Kangeyam

Kangeyam cows are drought-resistant and strong, often used for plowing and transport.
